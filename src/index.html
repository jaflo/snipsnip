Why?

Say you are making a video and have 3 minutes of footage but the song you want to use is 5 minutes long. You could cut the song at 3 minutes, but then it just ends. You could fade out the end, but it might be just after the beat drops. How anticlimactic... You could stich together the start and end of the song, which sounds more natural, but is tedious.

What does this do?

The song is analyzed to find two similar parts (like a repeating chorus), remove everything in between, and stitch together the two pieces. Done manually this is tedious since you need to listen to the entire thing and then match up the pieces, but kurz.app automates exactly that! Just upload an audio file and pick from potential matches.

What's up with the name?

kurz means short in German. And everything is an app nowadays. It makes for a short and spiffy URL.
